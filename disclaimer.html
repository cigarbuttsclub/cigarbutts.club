Welcome to a stock trading club that delivers you weekly picks of curated momentum stocks, and directs proceeds to charity. This way you get stocks with high-probability of appreciation in the short-term, whilst giving back to worthy causes - it's a win-win!

Here's how it works - every Saturday Cigarbutts Club generates a list of momentum stocks that we think are poised to move within the coming week or so (naturally there is no guarantee of this - see our disclaimer for more information). You can purchase access to a randomised selection of 3 stocks from the list, or access to the full list.

You won't find anything important here. Mostly, a catalogue of my thoughts on certain subjects in the form of essays and blog posts - I find that the process of writing helps to clarify, and structure my thinking. I'll also list the programs I develop with links to Github.
